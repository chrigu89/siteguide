The “SiteGuide” App from Trelleborg Pipe Seals is a collection of quality-related on-site tools for installers, specifiers, designers and deciders who use Trelleborg’s certified No-Dig DrainSystems in their trenchless pipe rehabilitation projects.

The app includes several resin calculators for the correct installation of liners and hat profiles according to rehabilitation method you are using as well as a broad range of downloadable technical data sheets, lists and tables — all of which are useful for project documentation needs and quality management. The Trelleborg SiteGuide App — your convenient on-site digital “field assistant”!
