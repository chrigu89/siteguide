The Trelleborg SiteGuide app provides comprehensive help for quality assurance to the pipe rehab professional using the different “No-Dig” Pipe rehabilitation methods from Trelleborg. Core element is the digital resin calculator for silicate and epoxy resin systems used for Trelleborg’s lateral connection and patch repair methods as well as for comprehensive rehabilitation methods, e.g. with Trelleborg’s Lining method (CIPP liners) or with the Main-to-House method for lateral connection lines.

Numerous downloads for templates of building site protocols make the app a practical, digital auxiliary tool on the building site.

Make this app yourself a comfortable digital "field assistant" on the project site.
